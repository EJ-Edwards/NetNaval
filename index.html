NetNaval

Battleship Game for Discord (Player vs Bot)

Overview

NetNaval is a turn-based Battleship game played entirely in Discord. The bot renders emoji-based boards and supports single-player (PvE) gameplay against an AI opponent.

Game Flow

  1. Start a new game
  2. Place your ships
  3. Fire at enemy coordinates
  4. Sink all enemy ships to win

Boards Explained

The game uses two different boards to ensure fair gameplay:

  • Your Board (Defense)
    Shows your ships, hits taken, and bot misses.
  • Radar Board (Offense)
    Shows only where you have fired: hits and misses. Enemy ships are never shown.
